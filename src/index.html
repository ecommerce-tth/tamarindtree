Nairobi Wilson Airport

Wilson Airport is one of the busiest airports in East and Central Africa. It's a fast and convenient gateway from Nairobi into Kenya’s magical interior lands including Maasai Mara, Mombasa, Amboseli, Lamu, Kilimanjaro, Diani, Lokichogio and Nanyuki.

Jomo Kenyatta International Airport

JKIA is Kenya’s largest aviation facility boasting over 40 passenger airlines & 25 cargo airlines and is the busiest airport in East & Central Africa. It’s importance as an aviation center makes it the pace setter for other airports in the region.

Nairobi National Park

Nairobi National Park is famous as the world's only wildlife capital. Wide open grass plains and backdrop of the city scrapers, scattered acacia bush play host to a wide variety of wildlife including the endangered black rhino, lions, leopards, cheetahs, hyenas, buffaloes, giraffes and diverse birdlife with over 400 species recorded.

The Karen Blixen Museum

The Karen Blixen Museum was once the centre piece of a farm at the foot of the Ngong Hills owned by Danish Author Karen and her Swedish Husband, Baron Bror Blixen-Finecke. The museum belongs to a different time period in the history of Kenya gaining international acclaim with the release of the Oscar winning movie ‘Out of Africa’.

Daphne Sheldrick Elephant Sanctuary

Nestled on the edge of Nairobi National Park lies a sanctuary for orphaned baby elephants, victims of the ivory poaching trade. The Daphne Sheldrick Elephant Orphanage rescues and rehabilitates elephants from all over Kenya.
